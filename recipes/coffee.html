The Perfect Cup of Coffee

Coffee recipe

Here is how to make the perfect cup of coffee.

  • 6oz boiling water
  • 2 tbsp prefered medium ground coffee
  • coffee filter
  • coffee mug

Place ground coffee in filter and place both into coffee mug. Pour boiling water over coffee grounds, and let steep for 3 minutes. Remove coffee filter, and allow excess fo drain. Enjoy!
